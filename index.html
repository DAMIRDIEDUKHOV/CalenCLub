Our team has created a calendar app that can track all of the meetings, due dates, and special events from clubs that a student joins.

Our app also tracks school assignments and can work as a personal calendar where you can input your own events or to-do lists.

Feature #1

CalenClub serves as a calendar for clubs to organize meeting dates, assignment due dates, competition dates, and any other dates that are pertaining to the club and its activities.

Feature #2

You can also track your club service hours, meeting hours, meeting attendance, assignment grades, competition results, and anything else that your club needs to track. This makes CalenClub a very good hub to keep track of everything club related.

Feature #3

CalenClub also allows for you to personalize your calendar to fit all of your personal dates and needs. This allows for you to make sure none of your clubs clash with other things that you need to complete, or appointments, etc.
